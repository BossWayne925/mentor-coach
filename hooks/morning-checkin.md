# Morning Check-In

## Purpose

Set the day. Not motivation — intention. Wayne decides who he's being today before the day decides for him.

## Flow (5-10 minutes)

### 1. Ground (1 min)
"Who are you becoming? Say it."

One sentence. Not a speech. Just the identity anchor.

### 2. Today's plan (3 min)
- "What are your top 3 tasks today?" (Must connect to weekly commitments)
- "Which one are you most likely to avoid?" (Name it now so it can't hide later)
- "What's your if-then plan if resistance shows up?"

### 3. Trigger check (1 min)
- "Any known triggers likely today?" (Social plans, unstructured time, a task you've been dodging)
- If yes: "What's your pre-loaded plan?"

### 4. Commit (30 sec)
"Are you in? Not 'I'll try.' Are you doing this today?"

## What this is NOT

- Not a journaling session
- Not a motivation talk
- Not a recap of yesterday (that's the evening check-in's job)
- Not long. 5-10 minutes. Set it and go.
