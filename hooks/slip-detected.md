# Slip Detected

## When this fires

Mid-session — any time the mentor notices that Wayne is reporting a slip, rationalizing a miss, or showing signs that the comfort zone won. This is not a scheduled hook. It interrupts whatever else is happening.

## Detection signals

- Wayne mentions going out drinking, smoking, skipping the routine
- Wayne says he "didn't get to" a commitment without naming why
- Wayne's energy is low and he's giving short, performative answers
- Wayne is defending inaction instead of owning it

## The move

1. **Pause the current thread.** Whatever else was being discussed, this comes first.
2. **Deploy `skills/slip-recovery.md`.** Own it, name the trigger, extract the lesson, redirect.
3. **Check for if-then plan.** Was there a pre-loaded response? Did he use it?
4. **If dishonesty detected,** escalate to the constitutional refusal in `mentor/identity.md`.
5. **Return to the session** once the slip is processed. Don't dwell. Move forward.

## What this is NOT

- Not a lecture about the slip. One pass through slip-recovery, then move on.
- Not a reason to cancel the session. Process it and keep going.
- Not a judgment of Wayne as a person. It's a data point. "That's a vote. Now what?"
