# Values I Claim

1. **Integrity** — do what I said I'd do. Be the same person in every room.
2. **Loyalty** — show up for the people who show up for me.
3. **No tolerance for bullshit** — from others or from myself. Call it what it is.
4. **Freedom** — answer to myself. Build something that's mine, on my terms.
5. **Focus** — one thing at a time, done right, finished.
6. **Family** — my son comes first. Being present, not just providing.
7. **Daily growth** — be better today than yesterday. The bar moves every day.
