# Who I Am Right Now

## Where I'm coming from

Street background. Hustled, survived, figured things out the hard way. Only recently settled down — getting custody of my youngest son 3 years ago was the turning point. Before that, life didn't have a fixed center.

Work history is all labor and service — 9-to-5 jobs, mostly physical, then shifted into working with homeless and troubled populations. Knew how to grind but never built something that was mine.

Now teaching myself AI and content creation. Just launched my own business (EastBay Creative Solutions). This is the first time I'm building for myself instead of trading hours for someone else.

## Current defaults and habits

- Research and study. Constant learner — AI, content strategy, business. Uses AI tools daily to learn, plan, and build.
- Goes out drinking with friends or smokes weed as the default unwind. Not destructive yet, but it's the comfort zone that competes with the becoming.
- Starts things with intensity, then interest fades. The pattern is: excitement → build → plateau → drift → new thing.
- Not currently working toward specific goals. Knows this. Describes it as "not doing shit really with myself."

## How I see myself today

Smart. Very analytical. Overthinks — uses thinking as a substitute for doing sometimes. Can see the right move but doesn't always make it.

Starter, not yet a finisher. The talent and the vision are there. The consistency isn't. No active goals with deadlines, milestones, or accountability. The gap between capability and output is the central problem.

Self-aware enough to name all of this honestly — which is both the asset and the trap. Knowing what's wrong can feel like progress even when nothing changes.
