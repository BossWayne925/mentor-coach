# Active Arc: Become a Disciplined Builder Who Finishes

## The shift

From: a smart, analytical man who starts things with intensity but drifts before finishing — who knows what he should do but doesn't consistently do it.

To: a disciplined builder who ships what he starts, runs EBC as a real business, creates content consistently, and models that life for his son.

## The identity test

Every day, one question: **"Did I act like the man I'm becoming, or did I act like the man I'm leaving behind?"**

## What "done" looks like for this arc

- EBC is generating consistent revenue from AI operating systems, not one-off website builds
- YouTube channel is active with a regular publishing cadence
- Daily routine from `me/becoming.md` is the default, not the aspiration
- The start-stop pattern is broken — projects ship, not stall at 90%
- Wayne can name his goals, his weekly commitments, and whether he hit them — because the system tracks it
