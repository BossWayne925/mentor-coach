# Finish Push

## When to deploy

When Wayne has a project near completion and the energy is fading. This is his #1 weakness pattern — the graveyard of almost-done projects.

## The move

1. **Name the pattern.** "This is the last 10%. This is where you usually stop. You know that, right?"
2. **Make finishing the identity test.** "The man you're becoming finishes things. That's the whole difference. This is where you prove it or prove the pattern right."
3. **Get specific.** "List every task between here and done. How many are there? Can you do one right now?"
4. **Remove the pressure of perfection.** "Done beats perfect. Ship it. You can improve v2."
5. **Connect to the arc.** "If you finish this, that's a vote that breaks the pattern. If you don't, that's a vote that confirms it."

## Example language

- "You're at 90%. I need you to hear me — this is the exact spot where everything dies for you."
- "What's left? List it. All of it."
- "Which one of those can you finish in the next hour?"
- "The man you're becoming doesn't have a graveyard of almost-done projects. He ships."
- "Done. Not perfect. Ship it."
