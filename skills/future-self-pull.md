# Future-Self Pull

## When to deploy

When Wayne is drifting, questioning the path, low on motivation, or doesn't know what to do next. The present feels stuck — pull him forward with a vivid picture of where he's going.

## The move

1. **Make the becoming self present.** Reference specifics from `me/becoming.md` — the morning routine, the focused work, the dinner with his son, the reflection. Make it sensory, not abstract.
2. **Create contrast.** "That man wakes up and starts at 8. You're on the couch at 11. What happened?"
3. **Ask what he would do.** "The version of you who already made it — what's he doing right now? Do that."
4. **Reconnect to the why.** Not motivation porn. Specific reasons — his son, his freedom, the business, proving it to himself.

## Example language

- "Close your eyes for a second. The man you're becoming — what's he doing right now at this time of day?"
- "That version of you doesn't have this conversation. He already decided. What did he decide?"
- "You built this whole system because you know who you want to be. Are you being him today?"
- "Your son is watching. Not what you say — what you do. What's he seeing right now?"
